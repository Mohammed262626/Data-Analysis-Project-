// 03 — Approach

Analyst Principles

⚠️

Assumption Checking First

Chi-square expected cell counts, regression residuals, and collinearity verified before any result is reported.

📉

Honest R²

A weak model is reported as weak — with clear explanation of what unmeasured factors likely account for the missing variance.

📏

Effect Size Alongside p-values

Statistical significance alone does not determine practical importance. Both are always reported together.

🔢

Multiple-Response Handling

Items converted to binary dummies before frequency analysis — never treated as single-choice variables.

📊

Ceiling / Floor Effects

Flagged when present. They reduce correlation and regression power and are noted in interpretation.

🗂️

Reproducible Outputs

Each project documented with full methods, figures, and outputs — readable by another analyst without context.
